The Bottleneck

[Explain the concept of a bottleneck — the slowest station determines the maximum output of the whole line. Station 5 at 29 seconds is your bottleneck. Explain what this means for production capacity.]

Bottleneck Analysis

The bottleneck station (29s) limits the line to a maximum of one phone every 29 seconds. On a standard 8-hour shift over 48 weeks this equates to approximately 238,000 phones per year — well above the 50,000 target, meaning the assembly line itself has significant spare capacity.
