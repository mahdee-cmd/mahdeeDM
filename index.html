About This Blog

This blog documents my learning throughout the 6E7V0013 Digital Manufacturing module at Manchester Metropolitan University.

Over the course of the semester I took on the role of a manufacturer of plastic casings, producing prototypes using various 3D printers, measuring them for dimensional accuracy, testing them on the FESTO CP assembly line, and modelling the entire production process in Tecnomatix Plant Simulation.

The target: prove that 50,000 phones can be assembled per year.
